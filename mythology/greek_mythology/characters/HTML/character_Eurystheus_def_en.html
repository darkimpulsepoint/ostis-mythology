Eurystheus - in ancient Greek mythology, the grandson of Perseus, the son of Sfenel and Nikippa, the cousin of Hercules, the king in Argolis. Serving Eurystheus, Hercules performed a number of feats for him. The body of Eurystheus is buried in Gargett, and the head is in Tricorinth near the source of Macarius, the place is called "Head of Eurystheus"
